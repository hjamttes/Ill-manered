Award-Winning Ohio Craft Beer

Since opening in Powell in 2015, Ill Mannered Brewing Company has focused on creating approachable, award-winning craft beer and welcoming spaces built around community.

Today, guests can visit both the original Powell brewery and the Uptown Marysville taproom, each offering a distinct atmosphere while sharing the same commitment to great beer and good company.
