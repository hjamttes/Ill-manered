Award-Winning Ohio Craft Beer

Ill Mannered Brewing Company is the collective brain child of the Ill Mannered Team: Ryan Romer-Jordan, Brian Mathias, Tom Ayers, and Greg Dannemiller — passionate home brewers who combined their varied backgrounds to build a unique brewing experience in the suburbs of Columbus.

The Powell Taproom is powered by a 6-barrel all-electric brewery, brewing just 186 gallons at a time so there's almost always something new to try. In 2024, the team opened a second Taproom & Barrel House in a restored 150+ year-old building in Uptown Marysville.
